What is web design?

Web design is the process of creating the layout and design of a website. It includes elements like color, fonts, structure, and user experience to create an appealing and functional site.

Why is digital marketing important?

Digital marketing helps businesses reach a wider audience online. It enables companies to promote products and services through various digital channels like social media, email, and search engines.

What is video editing?

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video shots to create a finished product. It includes cutting, adding effects, transitions, and sound to enhance the visual appeal of the video.

What is SEO and why is it important?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website to rank higher in search engine results. It helps increase visibility, drive traffic, and improve the overall online presence of a business.

Can I edit my website after it's built?

Yes! You can make changes to your website anytime. Many websites are built using content management systems (CMS) like WordPress, allowing easy editing even without technical knowledge.
